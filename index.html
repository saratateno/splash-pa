About

Stuck for something to do with your little one today? Yep. Us too.

When it feels like sanity is slipping away, we know it's a race against nap-time to get out of the house.

What's on? When? Will we even make it??

That's when we decided to build tiddlertime, a platform especially for parents of children under-5.

We'll help you find something great to do with your kids locally. And fast.

Let's stop Googling. Start going.
